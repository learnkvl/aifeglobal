Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes

We specialize in trading essential commodities including ICUMSA 45 sugar, premium coffee powder, high-quality rice varieties, aviation fuel (Jet A1), crude oil, and wheat. Our portfolio covers agricultural products, energy commodities, and food ingredients sourced from trusted suppliers worldwide.

Minimum order quantities vary by product and destination. For agricultural products like sugar and rice, we typically work with container loads (20-40 MT). For energy products like jet fuel and crude oil, minimum quantities are usually much larger. Contact us for specific MOQ requirements for your desired products.

We maintain strict quality control through certified laboratories, third-party inspections, and partnerships with reputable suppliers. All products come with certificates of analysis, and we offer pre-shipment inspection services. Our quality assurance team monitors every step from sourcing to delivery.

We offer flexible payment terms including Letters of Credit (LC), Documentary Collections, and bank guarantees. Payment terms are typically negotiated based on the relationship, order size, and destination. We work with major international banks to ensure secure transactions.

Yes, we provide comprehensive logistics solutions including shipping arrangements, customs clearance, and documentation. We work with trusted freight forwarders and shipping lines worldwide to ensure timely and cost-effective delivery. We can arrange both FOB and CIF terms based on your requirements.
